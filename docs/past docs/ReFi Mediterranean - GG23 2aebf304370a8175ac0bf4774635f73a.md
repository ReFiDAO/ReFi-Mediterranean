## **3Ô∏è‚É£ Funding Mechanism**

For this round, we're implementing [**ImpactQF**](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/citizen-grants-gcp-impact-passports-and-impact-quadratic-funding-impactqf/19712), alongside [Regen Coordination](https://www.regencoordination.xyz/regen-coordination-global-gg23), ‚Äì a new approach that rewards demonstrated impact.

**What is ImpactQF?** Our planned implementation combines:

1. **Impact Evaluation** ‚Äì A review and evaluation of previously completed activities, outputs and impact claims, rather than just forward-looking project proposals.
2. **Tunable Quadratic Funding (TQF)** ‚Äì A mechanism that enables impact evaluation scores to feed into the QF matching algorithm, adjusting how funds are distributed.

This hybrid approach aims to leverage the strengths of both models:

‚úÖ QF‚Äôs decentralized, community-driven funding allocation and crowdfunding

‚úÖ Retrospective evaluation of contributions and impact, ensuring results-driven funding

**What is Quadratic Funding (QF)?**

Quadratic Funding (QF) is a crowd-funding mechanism that amplifies available resources by inviting community members to make donations (big or small) that act as votes on where to allocate funds. The broader the support, the bigger the match!

https://www.wtfisqf.com/

**Why ImpactQF?**

We are using **ImpactQF** because we aim to experiment with a new mechanism with potential to:

- **Ensure funds reward impact** ‚Äì Projects receive funding based on results, not just well-written grant applications or social capital.
- **Reduce inefficiencies in traditional QF** ‚Äì Standard QF can be gamed by influencer-driven popularity; ImpactQF aims to balance community input with robust evaluation.
- **Strengthen transparency and trust** ‚Äì Karma GAP impact reports provide verifiable data, increasing accountability and legitimacy in ReFi grantmaking.
