## Theme Customizations

### Color Scheme
- **Mediterranean Ocean Theme** - Primary theme based on brand assets
- **Primary Dark:** `#0D4A6B` - Deep Mediterranean blue (ocean depths)
- **Primary Medium:** `#1E7A9F` - Mediterranean blue (sea surface)
- **Primary Light:** `#4AB3D9` - Light aqua (shallow waters)
- **Secondary:** `#D4A574` - Warm gold (sunset/sand)
- **Tertiary:** `#6B8E23` - Olive green (coastal vegetation)
- **Neutral:** `#F5F5DC` - Warm beige (sand/stone)

### Design Principles
- Ocean-inspired deep blues representing Mediterranean Sea
- Warm gold/terracotta accents representing Mediterranean sun/earth
- Natural palette with earth tones complementing blue
- Bioregional focus reflecting Mediterranean ecosystem
