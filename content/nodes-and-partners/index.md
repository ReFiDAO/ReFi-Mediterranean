# Nodes & Partners

ReFi Mediterranean brings together local nodes, partner networks, and aligned organizations working to advance regenerative finance across the Mediterranean bioregion.

## Member Organizations

ReFi Mediterranean coordinates with ReFi DAO Local Nodes and GreenPill Chapters across the Mediterranean. These nodes serve as local hubs for regenerative finance initiatives, connecting global Web3 innovations with place-based regeneration.
