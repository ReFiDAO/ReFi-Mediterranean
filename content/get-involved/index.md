# Get Involved

ReFi Mediterranean welcomes participation from projects, donors, partners, and nodes working to advance regenerative finance across the Mediterranean bioregion.

## For Projects

### Apply for Funding

ReFi Mediterranean runs bioregional funding rounds supporting regenerative projects. To apply:

1. **Complete Impact Reporting**: Document your activities and outputs in [Karma GAP](https://gap.karmahq.xyz/)
2. **Watch for Round Announcements**: Follow us on [Twitter](https://x.com/Refi_Med) for funding round announcements
3. **Apply During Open Windows**: Submit applications during announced application periods

**Eligibility**: Projects must be either:
- Established Web3/ReFi nodes in the Mediterranean bioregion (ReFi DAO Local Nodes, GreenPill Chapters)
- Local regenerators in the Mediterranean bioregion (land-based projects, communities with proven local impact)

### Integrate ReFi Tools

We help local regenerators integrate Web3/ReFi tools where they support mission. If you're interested in exploring how ReFi tools could support your project:

- Reach out via [Telegram](https://t.me/regenavocado) or [Twitter](https://x.com/Refi_Med)
- Review resources in our [Resources](/resources) section

## For Donors

### Support Funding Rounds

ReFi Mediterranean funding rounds use Quadratic Funding, which means:

- **Small donations matter**: Even $1 helps thanks to QF's matching algorithm
- **Broad support amplifies**: Projects with many supporters receive more matching funds
- **Impact-driven**: Our ImpactQF mechanism balances community input with rigorous evaluation

**How to Donate:**

1. Watch for round announcements on [Twitter](https://x.com/Refi_Med)
2. Visit Gitcoin during donation windows
3. Support your favorite projects within the ReFi Mediterranean round

## For Partners

### Matching Pool Partners

We collaborate with aligned networks and matching partners to grow shared capacity. Matching partners:

- Contribute to matching pools that amplify community donations
- Support innovative funding mechanisms like ImpactQF
- Help scale regenerative finance across the Mediterranean

### Network Partnerships

We partner with:

- **Regen Coordination**: ImpactQF mechanism experimentation
- **Karma GAP**: Impact reporting and evaluation framework
- **ReFi DAO**: Core steward and operations support
- **Local Nodes**: ReFi DAO Local Nodes and GreenPill Chapters

## For Nodes

### Join as a Member Organization

ReFi Mediterranean coordinates with ReFi DAO Local Nodes and GreenPill Chapters across the Mediterranean. Member organizations:

- Serve as local hubs for regenerative finance initiatives
- Connect global Web3 innovations with place-based regeneration
- Participate in bioregional funding rounds and gatherings
