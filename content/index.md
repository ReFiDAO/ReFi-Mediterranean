<div id="featured-project" class="callout-section">

## GG23 ImpactQF Round

*Our first bioregional-scale Quadratic Funding round supporting regenerative projects across the Mediterranean region.*

In partnership with Regen Coordination, we implemented **ImpactQF** ‚Äì a Tunable Quadratic Funding model that combines retrospective impact evaluation with Quadratic Funding. This innovative approach rewards demonstrated impact, not just well-written proposals.

The round allocated **$10,000 USDGLO** in matching funds to projects creating ecological, social, and economic impact across the Mediterranean bioregion.
