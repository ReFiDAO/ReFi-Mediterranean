# ReFi Mediterranean

Welcome to ReFi Mediterranean - a coordination of ReFi nodes serving the Mediterranean bioregion.

## About

ReFi Mediterranean connects regenerative finance initiatives across the Mediterranean region, supporting projects that leverage Web3 for real-world impact and address local needs.

## Our Mission

We're building bridges between the global ReFi movement and the Mediterranean context, focusing on:

- **Bioregional approaches** to financing regenerative initiatives
- **Local impact** supporting projects across Mediterranean countries
- **Community coordination** connecting local initiatives with global ReFi networks
- **Transparency** through open documentation and decision-making processes
