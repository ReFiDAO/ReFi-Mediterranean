# Events

ReFi Mediterranean convenes gatherings, funding rounds, and events that bring together regenerative projects, Web3 public goods communities, and funders across the Mediterranean bioregion.

## Past Events

### GG23 ImpactQF Round (April 2025)

**The first bioregional-scale Quadratic Funding round supporting regenerative projects across the Mediterranean region.**

This funding round ran from April 2-16, 2025, allocating $10,000 USDGLO in matching funds to projects creating ecological, social, and economic impact across the Mediterranean bioregion.

**Key Dates:**
- Applications opened: March 17, 2025
- Applications closed: April 1 @ noon UTC
- Donation window: April 2 @ noon UTC → April 16 @ 23:59 UTC
- Results announced: April 23, 2025

The round implemented **ImpactQF** – a Tunable Quadratic Funding model combining retrospective impact evaluation with Quadratic Funding. Projects were required to complete Activities & Outputs reporting in Karma GAP, ensuring funding rewarded demonstrated impact, not just proposals.
